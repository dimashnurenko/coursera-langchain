# Refund and Money-Back Policy

## 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a full refund within 30 days of your purchase if you're not completely satisfied with our service.

## Eligibility

### Who Qualifies
- All new customers on any paid plan
- Applies to first 30 days from subscription start date
- Available for monthly and annual subscriptions
- No questions asked - we want you to be happy

### Who Does NOT Qualify
- Free trial users (no payment made)
- Customers who have already received a refund in the past
- Renewals after the initial subscription period
- Add-on purchases made after 30 days

## Refund Process

### How to Request
1. Contact our support team at support@company.com
2. Provide your account email and reason for refund (optional)
3. We process requests within 2 business days
4. Refunds appear in your account within 5-10 business days

### What Gets Refunded
- Full subscription amount paid
- Any unused add-on purchases made with the subscription
- Pro-rated refunds not available (full 30-day period only)

## Important Notes

### Data Retention
- Account data is retained for 30 days after refund
- You can re-subscribe within 30 days without data loss
- After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted

### Limitations
- Only one refund per customer per year
- Refund does not apply to third-party integrations
- Services used extensively may be reviewed (abuse prevention)
