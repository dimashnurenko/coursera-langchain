# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Account Management

### How do I upgrade my account?
You can upgrade from your account settings page. Go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately and you'll only pay the prorated difference.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your data before canceling or re-subscribe within 30 days to restore full access.

## Billing and Payments

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and wire transfer for annual enterprise plans.

### Can I get an invoice?
Yes! Invoices are automatically sent to your billing email after each payment. You can also download invoices from Settings > Billing > Invoice History.

### Do you offer discounts?
- Annual plans: 15% discount compared to monthly
- Educational institutions: 25% discount (verification required)
- Non-profits: 30% discount (501(c)(3) verification required)
- Teams of 50+: Volume discounts available - contact sales

## Technical Support

### What are your support hours?
- Email support: 24/7 for all plans
- Live chat: Mon-Fri 9am-6pm EST for Premium and Enterprise
- Phone support: Enterprise plans only, Mon-Fri 8am-8pm EST

### How quickly do you respond to support tickets?
- Free plan: Within 48 hours
- Basic plan: Within 24 hours
- Premium plan: Within 4 hours
- Enterprise plan: Within 1 hour

### Do you offer implementation assistance?
Yes! Premium and Enterprise plans include onboarding assistance. Enterprise plans also include dedicated account management and quarterly business reviews.

## Features and Usage

### Is there a file size limit?
- Free plan: 100MB per file
- Basic plan: 500MB per file
- Premium plan: 10GB per file
- Enterprise plan: Unlimited

### How many team members can I add?
- Free plan: Just you (single user)
- Basic plan: Up to 5 team members
- Premium plan: Unlimited team members
- Enterprise plan: Unlimited + advanced admin controls

### Can I integrate with other tools?
Yes! We have integrations with Slack, Microsoft Teams, Google Workspace, Salesforce, and many more. Premium and Enterprise plans also include API access for custom integrations.

## Security and Privacy

### Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We're SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified.

### Where is my data stored?
Data is stored in secure data centers in the US, EU, and Asia-Pacific. Enterprise customers can choose their data region for compliance reasons.

### Do you sell my data?
Never. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data belongs to you.
